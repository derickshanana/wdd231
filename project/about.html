About Us

GrowLocal Community Gardens is dedicated to promoting sustainable gardening practices and fostering community engagement through education, collaboration, and hands-on gardening experiences.

Our History

Founded in 2020, GrowLocal Community Gardens has grown from a small neighborhood initiative to a city-wide movement with multiple garden locations and a vibrant community of gardeners.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate volunteers, expert gardeners, and community leaders who work together to make our gardens thrive.

Volunteers

Our dedicated volunteers help with planting, maintenance, and community events.

Community Leaders

Our community leaders guide our projects and help build strong community relationships.

Expert Gardeners

Our expert gardeners provide valuable knowledge and skills to ensure our gardens thrive.
